Defensive Pedal Preview v0.2.22

WHAT'S NEW

1. Fewer pre-ride weather warnings.
   - No warning when temperature stays between 10 and 27 C.
   - Warns about cold (under 5 C), heat (over 30 C, NEW), or big swings (over 13 C in the day).
   - All warning copy now advises caution instead of telling you to postpone the ride.

2. Top Contributors and the Neighborhood Leaderboard now hide anonymous (not-signed-up) riders.
   Their trips still count toward community-wide totals on City Heartbeat — they just don't
   appear in the named ranking until they sign up with Google or email.

3. Signed-in users always start on a clean route-planning screen on cold start.
   No more reopening the app to a stale route preview from yesterday.
   (Anonymous users keep the resume flow as part of the signup prompt.)

4. Trip duplicates fixed. The same ride no longer appears multiple times in History.
   Server-side idempotency: any retry from the offline queue (timeout, app kill,
   dropped response) now upserts instead of inserting a duplicate row.
