PRE-RELEASE SMOKE CHECKLIST  (Phase 4, error-reduction plan)

Do all of the following on the just-built APK before promoting the build
beyond your own device. Sign in with a fresh anonymous session AND a real
Google account. Test on at least one preview-track install.

  [ ] Cold launch — app reaches route-planning within ~3s, no blank screen.
  [ ] Plan a route — destination autocomplete works; Safe/Fast/Flat toggle
       refetches; preview screen shows risk/elevation/weather.
  [ ] Start navigation — 3D follow camera engages; HUD shows distance/ETA;
       hazard alerts surface if any nearby.
  [ ] End ride — End-Ride dialog appears; Save/Discard branches both reach
       feedback or route-planning without freezing; ride lands in History.
  [ ] View trips — History tab loads list; tapping a card opens the replay
       map with the GPS trail rendered.
  [ ] Community — Community feed loads without permission prompts on the
       second visit; City Heartbeat dashboard renders.
  [ ] Sign in / sign out — Google sign-in succeeds (no `…supabase.co` URL
       visible to the user); sign-out clears profile + returns to anonymous.

  [ ] (production AAB only) Verify cert owner matches the upload keystore.
       Without `DEFPEDAL_UPLOAD_*` set, Gradle silently falls back to debug
       signing and Play will reject the bundle.
  [ ] (production AAB only) Wait for the rollout to go live BEFORE
       updating the Data Safety form. Hard rule: form change AFTER live
       AAB — see CLAUDE.md > Play Store Release.

If any box is unchecked, do NOT promote past 5%. File a bug, fix on a new
build, re-run the checklist from the top.
