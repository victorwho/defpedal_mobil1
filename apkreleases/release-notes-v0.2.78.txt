v0.2.78 — Pedal Nudge System (preview)

This build contains the mobile-facing pieces of a Duolingo-style retention
engine voiced by Pedal. The server side (cron + push delivery) isn't
deployed yet, so nudges WON'T actually arrive on your phone in this
preview — but the in-app surfaces are all here for visual review.

What to test:

  - Post-ride streak +1 animation
    Save any ride end-to-end. The impact summary card now opens with a
    new tier-aware streak block at the top: a colored flame icon that
    flickers, the streak number that springs in from a 0.9 scale, and
    the day count. Color tier should follow:
      Day 1-6     yellow flame
      Day 7-20    orange (Spark)
      Day 21-41   red (Commute Habit)
      Day 42-87   blue
      Day 88-99   purple
      Day 100-364 gold
      Day 365+    rainbow gold

  - Refreshed Streak card on Impact dashboard
    Go to History tab > Impact. The streak card now uses the same flame
    atom plus a tier label ("Spark", "Commute Habit", "Century" etc.)
    in accent yellow under the day count. Flame animates gently when
    streak >= 1.

  - Meet Pedal one-time onboarding card
    For testers signed in already with at least one saved ride, the
    card may not appear (you've already "seen" it implicitly because
    completed-ride count is >= 1 and the flag hadn't been set when you
    first saved). Easiest way to see it: dev variant, fresh install,
    save your first ride. A modal pops up: hero Pedal wave pose, title
    "Meet Pedal", body explaining the voice, "Got it" dismisses.
    Settings: hasSeenMeetPedalCard persists, so it never repeats.

  - Profile > Pedal Nudges section
    Profile screen has a new section between Display and Account. Two
    toggles:
      - Pedal voice (sassy/neutral) — flips a server-synced flag that
        the cron will read when picking message variants once we ship
        the back-end.
      - Streak reminders — category opt-out for all streak-related
        nudges (mirrors profiles.notify_streak server-side).

What's NOT in this build:

  - The actual nudges. They're written and tested but not delivered
    yet. The cron endpoint, the priority queue, the message catalog,
    the safety floor (no pushes in bad weather or after dark), the
    streak-loss apology, the lapsed re-engagement, the rally-friends
    share — all server-side, all tested in mocha/vitest, all dark
    until ops wires the Cloud Scheduler jobs.

Please confirm:

  - The streak animation is delightful, not distracting
  - The tier label is readable
  - The Pedal Nudges section feels at home alongside the existing
    Display rows (not jarring)
  - The Meet Pedal modal copy reads right (especially "I keep things
    light" — does it land or feel forced?)

Known gaps:

  - "Rainbow" flame for the Legend tier (365+ days) renders as
    saturated gold for now. The multi-stop gradient SVG is a polish
    item not in this build.
  - We don't ship the Pedal pose poses for "podium" and "legend"
    tiers yet; the atom falls back to existing trophy/excited art.
