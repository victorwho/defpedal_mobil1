v0.2.31 — Landscape on non-map screens

Profile, History, Community, Trophy Case, Settings, FAQ, Diagnostics,
Onboarding and Auth now rotate when the OS auto-rotate is on. Tilt your
phone or use it on a stand and the layout follows.

Map and navigation screens stay portrait — they're built for handlebar
mounts and locked deliberately. Tilting the phone mid-ride won't flip
the route, the camera, or the maneuver card.

Other improvements:

  - Trophy Case grid expands from 3 columns to 5 in landscape, so badges
    fill the screen instead of leaving big gutters.

  - The intermittent "Invalid Refresh Token" log/yellowbox you may have
    seen on app open is now silent. The recovery (anonymous sign-in
    falls through) was always working — just the noisy log message is
    suppressed at the source.

  - Cleanup follow-ups from v0.2.30: every fix in that release ships in
    this build too, so this is a strict superset.

Note for testers running v0.2.30: the rotation behavior is a native
change, so you must install this APK to see it — hot reload alone can't
ship it. Once installed, make sure your phone's auto-rotate setting is
on (system shade), and the rotatable screens will follow.

If you mount the phone sideways for any reason and want a screen to
stay landscape, that's tier-2 territory: map screens still need a
landscape redesign before they support it. Let us know if that matters
to you and we'll prioritize it.
