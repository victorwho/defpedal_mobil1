v0.2.29 — Trips now refresh on focus

Fixes

The DB partial-index bug from session 33 was fixed yesterday (server-side
migration), so trip_start / trip_end / trip_track now succeed. But the
History screen's React Query cache had `staleTime: 60s` and didn't
refetch on tab switch, so trips synced AFTER you last looked at History
stayed invisible until the cache went stale.

Now the History screen calls refetch() every time it gains focus.
Switching to Profile and back, or coming out of feedback, immediately
shows the latest trips.

Recovery for trips lost in the 2026-04-27 → 2026-05-05 window:

- Open Diagnostics → tap "Retry dead mutations" (you already did this).
- Then go to History — the orphaned trips will appear once the queue
  drains (within ~15 seconds; tab away and back to force a refetch).
- Force-quit the app and reopen if any trips still don't show.

New rides going forward: complete a ride, watch the queue in
Diagnostics drain to zero (~15s), then check History — the trip
appears immediately when the screen gains focus.
