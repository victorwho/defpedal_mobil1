Defensive Pedal Preview v0.2.91 (build 94)

This build ships the fixes from the full-app review (Phases 0-4). Highlights to test:

RIDE FLOW
- Lock-screen recording: lock the phone and keep riding for a few minutes,
  then unlock — the distance you covered while locked is now counted.
- Swipe the app away from recents mid-ride: the tracking notification now
  stops (no more lingering notification / battery drain).
- End Ride now has a "Keep riding" option; tapping outside the dialog keeps
  riding instead of ending. Android back during a ride opens End Ride instead
  of dropping you to a dead screen.

ACCOUNTS
- New "Forgot password?" on the email sign-in screen (reset by email link).

ONBOARDING & LANGUAGE
- The whole onboarding flow is now translated (try Romanian / Spanish in
  Profile > Display).
- After your first ride, the celebration pop-ups (badges, rank-up, meet Pedal)
  now appear ONE AT A TIME instead of stacking.

PRIVACY / SECURITY (under the hood)
- Shared-route links no longer expose the sharer's exact start/end address.
- Community comments are moderated and rate-limited consistently.

PERFORMANCE & POLISH
- Smoother during navigation (fewer background writes), faster Trophy Case,
  better text scaling on large font sizes, screen-reader labels on the route
  sheet, localized lock-screen notification.

Please report anything that looks off — especially around ending a ride,
lock-screen tracking, and the onboarding flow in Romanian.
